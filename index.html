I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer who spends most of my time on the backend — designing data models, building REST APIs, and wiring systems together so they stay reliable once real people depend on them.

Over the past four years at Neurosoft Technologies Ltd., I've built and shipped a government project appraisal platform for the Planning Ministry of Bangladesh, a VAT management system handling regulatory calculations, and a hire-purchase management system deployed across 500+ branches of Singer Bangladesh Ltd. with live ERP and national ID verification integrations .

Lately I've been going deep on modern async Python — SQLAlchemy with asyncpg, JWT auth backed by Redis, and Celery task queues — while staying close to the Frappe/ERPNext ecosystem that most of my production experience is built on.
